Tips for Success

  • Time‑block: Stick to two focused sessions with breaks.
  • Commit daily: Push to GitHub each evening with clear messages.
  • Ask for help: Try 30 min on your own, then seek help on StackOverflow/Discord.
  • Reflect: End each day writing 3 things you learned and 1 question.
